2.3 - Strengths and weakness of my final project


Strengths - I am very much pleased with the outcome of my website that I have created. The website is simple to read (with font downloaded) and navigation between each web page created. The text structure is paragraphed into each section with two different colours (white + pastel green) and headings for each section, makes it easier to read. The navigation bar I have created so it’s easy to navigate to other pages after reading the content, each link selected appears active when clicked on. Weakness - The background image, as it doesn’t really show what it is (maybe a plain colour to make web page look brighter or a better positioned image). Better position of the nav bar, with no gap between nav bar and the image above the nav bar (so it looks more visually appealing).
