1.1 - Describe purpose of IT in modern business

Innovation, rapid growth and better communication provides a business with the importance of keeping the business afloat and engaged to keep up with client/customer demand for their product or service. Communication in business is essential to operate and keep data/information secure, having digital servers means data is easy to maintain with less paper files. [1]

1.4 - Risks that might impact digital projects


In business most risks are not accounted for within a project e.g. a business will run over-budget, over-time and consistently not deliver the intended outcome. It’s not easy when you to/don’t have ironclad answers to every question as things can change quickly. There are many factors on what causes digital projects to go wrong e.g. 6 months into a website build Apple releases a new version of iOS and the website doesn’t work properly (updated versions of web browsers can also cause issues). Failure to plan the project regardless of the size or nature would result in a business spending more days putting out fires, investing time and addressing risks would benefit a business to prepare for unexpected events that might occur.[2]

GDPR (General data protection regulation) conducts how businesses use personal data to analyse customers with digital marketing activities, this becomes more challenging for a business. Gathering information from customers interactions process customers choices of improving products on specific needs. Customers are targeted through one-to-one communications e.g. email, SMS, instant messaging in groups or segments. [3]
